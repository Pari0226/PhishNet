🛡️ Cyber Awareness

Stay informed. Stay protected. Your first defense against phishing is knowledge.

🔗 Don’t click suspicious links

Avoid clicking on links from unknown emails or messages. Always verify the sender before proceeding.

🔒 Look for HTTPS

Ensure websites use secure protocols. Always check if the URL begins with “https://” and shows a lock icon.

👀 Be cautious of urgency

Phishing messages often create panic — e.g., “Your account will be closed!” Take a moment to evaluate before reacting.

🧠 Think before you act

If something feels off, it probably is. Don’t share OTPs, passwords, or personal details without verifying the platform.

📞 Contact official support

When in doubt, contact the company or service through their verified website or helpline.
